Our Investment Focus

Exceed Growth Partners concentrates primarily on acquiring and investing in established B2B services companies where our operational experience and strategic approach can drive significant value. Our deep understanding of service-based models allows us to be effective partners in this sector.

Opportunistic Investments: While our core expertise and primary focus lie in B2B Services, we opportunistically pursue investments in profitable, well-managed small businesses across other select industries where our operational approach and partnership model can foster significant growth and enduring value, typically through our Direct Investment strategy.

Ideal Attributes:

Target Companies

Profitable B2B services businesses with $1M+ EBITDA, strong margins, and established market presence. We selectively consider profitable businesses outside B2B services where our operational expertise applies.

Growth Potential

Companies with demonstrated growth and identifiable opportunities for operational enhancement. We focus on businesses at inflection points where we can accelerate their success and drive value creation.

Recurring Revenue

Business models featuring predictable, recurring revenue streams with diverse customer bases (no client >20% of revenue). We prioritize companies demonstrating strong client retention and low churn.

Management

Strong existing leadership teams open to partnership and strategic guidance. We also consider businesses with capable second-in-command executives who can assume leadership with the exiting seller's support.

Our Flexible Strategy

Exceed Growth Partners provides tailored capital solutions combined with deep operational expertise to support the growth and transition of compelling B2B services companies. We recognize that every business situation is unique, and our flexible approach allows us to structure partnerships that best align with the specific objectives of owners, management teams, and financing partners. Our strategy typically involves two primary models:

Direct Investments

Focus: For select profitable companies, primarily within B2B services but opportunistically in other sectors where we see a strong fit, Exceed seeks to be the sole or lead equity partner. This often involves companies in the lower middle market where our hands-on approach can make a significant impact. This typically involves acquiring a majority interest, providing a thoughtful transition path for owners, and partnering closely with existing leadership teams or helping recruit new talent.

Approach: Our emphasis is on providing patient, long-term capital combined with hands-on operational support. We collaborate actively with management to drive sustainable growth, implement scalable processes, enhance efficiency, and build upon the company's established foundation and legacy.

Strategic Co-Investments

Focus: In situations typically involving larger companies or those already partnered with private equity firms, venture capital groups, or family offices (generally within our core B2B services focus areas), Exceed pursues strategic co-investment and advisory opportunities.

Approach: We earn valuable investment rights alongside lead sponsors by contributing distinct strategic or operational value. This model allows portfolio companies and their lead investors to gain immediate access to Exceed's targeted expertise, accelerating key value creation initiatives and enhancing operational execution.

Conclusion: Through both direct investments and strategic co-investments, Exceed functions as a operationally-focused growth partner committed to building enduring value within the B2B services sector and beyond.
